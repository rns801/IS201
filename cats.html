How to care for a cat

  1. Get plenty of cat food and water
    • Many cats prefer their food and water to be separate
    • Stainless steel bowls can help reduce cat acne
  2. Provide a cozy home environment, with lots of toys and blankets
    • Cats like to be up high, and to have a place to hide, so provide both
  3. Play with your cat
    • Cats can play fetch with toy mice or crumpled receipts
    • Don't let your cat play with string unattended, they can swallow it
